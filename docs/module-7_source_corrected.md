# DERS 41 — Was / Were

## 1. Ders Adı
Was and Were  
(“am / is / are” yapısının geçmiş hâli)

## 2. Öğrenme Hedefleri
- Geçmişte bir kişinin veya şeyin durumunu anlatmak.
- `I / he / she / it` ile `was` kullanmak.
- `you / we / they` ile `were` kullanmak.
- `wasn't / weren't` ile olumsuz cümle kurmak.
- `Was...? / Were...?` ile basit soru sormak.

## 3. Ön Bilgiler
- Subject pronouns
- `am / is / are`
- Olumlu, olumsuz ve soru yapıları
- Zaman ifadeleri: yesterday, last night, last week

## 4. Türkçe Konu Anlatımı
`Was` ve `were`, `am / is / are` yapısının geçmiş hâlidir.

- I am tired today. → I **was** tired yesterday.
- She is at school now. → She **was** at school yesterday.
- They are happy now. → They **were** happy yesterday.

Kural:

- I / He / She / It → **was**
- You / We / They → **were**

Olumsuz:

- was not → **wasn't**
- were not → **weren't**

Soru:

- Was she at home?
- Were they tired?

## 5. Kolaydan Zora 10 Örnek
1. I was at home yesterday.
2. She was happy after the match.
3. He was tired last night.
4. It was cold in the morning.
5. We were at school yesterday.
6. They were in the sports hall.
7. Beyza wasn't late.
8. The players weren't tired.
9. Was the coach at the match?
10. Were the microphones on the stage?

## 6. Kural Tablosu
| Özne | Olumlu | Olumsuz | Soru |
|---|---|---|---|
| I / He / She / It | was | wasn't | Was ...? |
| You / We / They | were | weren't | Were ...? |

## 7. Sık Yapılan Hatalar
- `I were tired.` ❌ → `I was tired.` ✅
- `They was happy.` ❌ → `They were happy.` ✅
- `She didn't was at home.` ❌ → `She wasn't at home.` ✅
- `Was they ready?` ❌ → `Were they ready?` ✅

# DERS 42 — Past Simple Regular Verbs

## 1. Ders Adı
Past Simple — Regular Verbs  
(Düzenli fiillerle geçmiş zaman)

## 2. Öğrenme Hedefleri
- Geçmişte tamamlanan eylemleri anlatmak.
- Düzenli fiillere `-ed` eklemek.
- Temel yazım değişikliklerini fark etmek.
- `yesterday`, `last week`, `two days ago` gibi zaman ifadelerini kullanmak.

## 3. Ön Bilgiler
- Present Simple olumlu cümleler
- Temel fiiller
- Was / were

## 4. Türkçe Konu Anlatımı
Geçmişte başlayıp bitmiş bir eylemi Past Simple ile anlatırız.

Düzenli fiiller genellikle `-ed` alır:

- play → played
- watch → watched
- clean → cleaned

Sonu `e` ile biten fiiller yalnız `-d` alır:

- like → liked
- live → lived

Sonu sessiz harf + `y` ile biten bazı fiillerde:

- study → studied
- carry → carried

Kural:

**Özne + fiilin geçmiş hâli**

- I played volleyball yesterday.
- She watched a film last night.

## 5. Kolaydan Zora 10 Örnek
1. I played volleyball yesterday.
2. She watched a film last night.
3. He cleaned his room.
4. We visited our grandparents.
5. They listened to music.
6. Beyza studied English after school.
7. The coach talked to the players.
8. The students prepared the stage.
9. The team practised for two hours.
10. We carried the chairs into the hall.

## 6. Kural Tablosu
| Fiil tipi | Kural | Örnek |
|---|---|---|
| Çoğu fiil | + ed | play → played |
| Sonu e | + d | live → lived |
| Sessiz + y | y → ied | study → studied |

## 7. Sık Yapılan Hatalar
- `playd` ❌ → `played` ✅
- `studyed` ❌ → `studied` ✅
- `She played volleyball yesterday` cümlesinde fiile ayrıca `-s` gelmez.
- `I was played` ❌ → `I played` ✅

# DERS 43 — Past Simple Irregular Verbs

## 1. Ders Adı
Past Simple — Irregular Verbs  
(Düzensiz fiiller)

## 2. Öğrenme Hedefleri
- Sık kullanılan düzensiz fiillerin geçmiş hâllerini öğrenmek.
- `go → went`, `have → had`, `see → saw` gibi temel dönüşümleri kullanmak.
- Geçmişte tamamlanan olayları anlatmak.
- Düzenli ve düzensiz fiilleri ayırt etmek.

## 3. Ön Bilgiler
- Past Simple düzenli fiiller
- Temel günlük fiiller
- Zaman ifadeleri

## 4. Türkçe Konu Anlatımı
Bazı fiiller geçmiş zamanda `-ed` almaz. Biçimleri değişir:

- go → went
- have → had
- see → saw
- eat → ate
- drink → drank
- take → took
- come → came
- get → got
- make → made
- buy → bought

Bu fiiller ezberlenir; ancak tek seferde hepsini değil, sık kullanılanları öğrenmek yeterlidir.

Örnek:

- I went to school yesterday.
- She had breakfast at eight.
- We saw a good match.

## 6. Kural Tablosu
| Fiil | Geçmiş hâli |
|---|---|
| go | went |

## 7. Sık Yapılan Hatalar
- `goed` ❌ → `went` ✅

# DERS 44 — Did / Didn't

## 1. Ders Adı
Past Simple — Did and Didn't  
(Geçmiş zamanda soru ve olumsuzluğa hazırlık)

## 2. Öğrenme Hedefleri
- `didn't + fiilin yalın hâli` ile olumsuz cümle kurmak.
- `Did + özne + fiilin yalın hâli?` yapısını tanımak.
- Geçmiş fiilin `did/didn't` sonrasında yalın hâle döndüğünü fark etmek.
- Düzenli ve düzensiz fiillerle olumsuz cümleler kurmak.

## 3. Ön Bilgiler
- Past Simple olumlu cümleler
- Düzenli ve düzensiz fiiller
- Present Simple `do / does`

## 4. Türkçe Konu Anlatımı
Past Simple olumsuz cümlelerinde `didn't` kullanırız:

- I played. → I **didn't play**.
- She went. → She **didn't go**.

Çok önemli:

`didn't` geldikten sonra fiil geçmiş hâlde kalmaz; **yalın hâle döner**.

- didn't went ❌
- didn't go ✅

Soruya hazırlık:

- Did you play?
- Did she go?

7. The players didn't arrive late.
8. The coach didn't bring a new ball.

## 6. Kural Tablosu
| Cümle türü | Kalıp | Örnek |
|---|---|---|
| Olumlu | geçmiş fiil | She went. |
| Olumsuz | didn't + yalın fiil | She didn't go. |
| Soru | Did + özne + yalın fiil? | Did she go? |

## 7. Sık Yapılan Hatalar
- `She didn't went.` ❌ → `She didn't go.` ✅
- `They didn't played.` ❌ → `They didn't play.` ✅
- `He not watched TV.` ❌ → `He didn't watch TV.` ✅
- `Did she went?` ❌ → `Did she go?` ✅

# DERS 45 — Past Simple Questions and Short Answers

## 1. Ders Adı
Past Simple — Questions and Short Answers  
(Geçmiş zamanda sorular ve kısa cevaplar)

## 2. Öğrenme Hedefleri
- `Did + özne + fiilin yalın hâli?` ile soru kurmak.
- `Yes, ... did. / No, ... didn't.` ile kısa cevap vermek.
- `What / Where / When / Who` ile geçmiş zaman soruları oluşturmak.
- Soru cümlesinde fiili yalın kullanmak.

## 3. Ön Bilgiler
- Did / didn't
- Past Simple olumlu cümleler
- Soru kelimeleri

## 4. Türkçe Konu Anlatımı
Past Simple soru kalıbı:

**Did + özne + fiilin yalın hâli?**

- Did you play volleyball?
- Did she go to school?

Kısa cevap:

- Yes, I did.
- No, she didn't.

Soru kelimeleri:

- What did you eat?
- Where did they go?
- When did she arrive?
- Who did you call?

`Did` varsa fiil yine yalın hâlde kalır.

## 5. Kolaydan Zora 10 Örnek
1. Did you watch TV?
2. Did she play volleyball?
3. Did they go to school?
4. Did he eat breakfast?
5. Where did you go?
6. What did Beyza study?

8. Who did the coach call?
9. Did the students prepare the stage?
10. What did they buy for the event?

## 6. Kural Tablosu
| Soru tipi | Kalıp | Örnek |
|---|---|---|
| Evet/Hayır | Did + özne + fiil? | Did you go? |
| Bilgi sorusu | Soru kelimesi + did + özne + fiil? | Where did you go? |
| Kısa cevap | Yes, ... did. / No, ... didn't. | Yes, I did. |

## 7. Sık Yapılan Hatalar
- `Did she went?` ❌ → `Did she go?` ✅
- `Where you did go?` ❌ → `Where did you go?` ✅
- `Yes, she went.` kısa cevap olarak kullanılabilir ama hedef kalıp `Yes, she did.` olmalıdır.

# DERS 46 — Telling a Past Story

## 1. Ders Adı
Telling a Past Story  
(Geçmişte yaşanan bir olayı sıralı anlatma)

## 2. Öğrenme Hedefleri
- Geçmişte yaşanan olayları sıraya koymak.
- `first, then, next, after that, finally` bağlaçlarını kullanmak.
- Düzenli ve düzensiz geçmiş zaman fiillerini birlikte kullanmak.
- 5–6 cümlelik kısa bir geçmiş zaman hikâyesi oluşturmak.

## 3. Ön Bilgiler
- Was / were
- Past Simple düzenli ve düzensiz fiiller
- Did / didn't
- Zaman sıralama kelimeleri

## 4. Türkçe Konu Anlatımı
Bir geçmiş zaman hikâyesi anlatırken olayları sıraya koymak gerekir.

Kullanışlı kelimeler:

- **First** → İlk önce
- **Then** → Sonra
- **Next** → Ardından
- **After that** → Bundan sonra
- **Finally** → Son olarak

Örnek:

First, we went to the sports hall.  
Then, we changed our shoes.  
Next, we warmed up.  
After that, we played a match.  
Finally, we went home.

## 5. Kolaydan Zora 10 Örnek

2. Then, I had breakfast.
3. Next, I went to school.
4. After that, I studied English.
5. Finally, I went home.
6. First, the team arrived at the hall.
7. Then, the players warmed up.
8. Next, they played two sets.
9. After that, they drank water.
10. Finally, the coach talked to the team.

## 6. Kural Tablosu
| Sıra | Bağlaç | Örnek |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | First | First, we arrived. |
| 2 | Then | Then, we practised. |
| 3 | Next | Next, we played. |
| 4 | After that | After that, we rested. |
| Son | Finally | Finally, we went home. |

## 7. Sık Yapılan Hatalar
- Olayları zaman sırası olmadan karışık anlatmak

# DERS 47 — Past Continuous

## 1. Ders Adı
Past Continuous  
(Geçmişte belirli bir anda devam eden eylem)

## 2. Öğrenme Hedefleri
- `was / were + verb-ing` yapısını kullanmak.
- Geçmişte belirli bir anda devam eden eylemleri anlatmak.
- `at five o'clock`, `at that moment`, `while` gibi ifadeleri tanımak.
- Past Simple ile Past Continuous arasındaki temel farkı görmek.

## 3. Ön Bilgiler
- Was / were
- Present Continuous
- Past Simple
- `-ing` yazım kuralları

## 4. Türkçe Konu Anlatımı
Past Continuous, geçmişte belirli bir anda devam eden eylemi anlatır.

Kural:

**Özne + was / were + fiil-ing**

- I was studying at eight.
- She was playing volleyball.
- They were listening to music.

Özneye göre:

- I / He / She / It → was
- You / We / They → were

Kısa fark:

- I studied English yesterday. → Dün İngilizce çalıştım. (tamamlanmış eylem)
- I was studying English at eight. → Saat sekizde İngilizce çalışıyordum. (o anda sürüyordu)

## 5. Kolaydan Zora 10 Örnek
1. I was reading at eight.
2. She was sleeping at ten.
3. He was doing his homework.
4. We were watching a match.
5. They were playing volleyball.
6. Beyza was carrying her sports bag.
7. The coach was talking to the team.
8. The students were preparing the stage.
9. While the players were warming up, the coach was checking the net.
10. At that moment, everyone was listening carefully.

## 6. Kural Tablosu
| Özne | Yapı | Örnek |
|---|---|---|
| I / He / She / It | was + ing | She was reading. |
| You / We / They | were + ing | They were playing. |

## 7. Sık Yapılan Hatalar
- `She were reading.` ❌ → `She was reading.` ✅
- `They was playing.` ❌ → `They were playing.` ✅
- `I was read.` ❌ → `I was reading.` ✅
- `We were watched TV.` ❌ → `We were watching TV.` ✅

# MODÜL 7 GRAMMAR KAPSAM KONTROLÜ

- `was / were` olumlu, olumsuz ve soru yapıları işlendi.
- Past Simple düzenli ve temel düzensiz fiiller ayrı derslerde öğretildi.
- `didn't` ve `Did...?` yapılarından sonra fiilin yalın hâle dönmesi açıkça işlendi.

- Past Continuous yalnız `was / were + -ing` ve belirli anda devam eden eylem düzeyinde işlendi.
- Past Simple ile Past Continuous farkı başlangıç düzeyinde tutuldu.
- Present Perfect ve ileri düzey zaman ilişkileri kullanılmadı.
- İçerik 10–11 yaş düzeyine uygun, kısa ve doğrudan tutuldu.
